As the majority of people aren't aware of exactly what kind of illness/pain/discomfort they are facing. We tend to provide them with a better perspective about it. Our solution works on identifying the pin-point location where the user is having issues, as they point their finger towards it. Also, with the help of customised questions we recommend probable medical issues the user might be suffering with. Main focus of the website is to, recommend the best possible Medical Practitioner following the medical issue found. Also, we generate a report based on the information provided by the user to the Doctor / Medical Practitioner for ease of understanding and efficient conversation.
